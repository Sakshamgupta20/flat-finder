Are you a student studying in North London and in need of some luxury student accommodation? Look no further! Our stunning Charles Morton Court offers a mix of stylish en suite rooms and studios that are just a short stroll away from Canonbury Station (zone 2).

With central London at your fingertips, you can explore this magical city with ease. Charles Morton Court is super close to London Metropolitan University, City University London, and Central Saint Martins – giving you a quick and easy commute!

Our bright, airy, and spacious student homes offer plenty of storage space for all your belongings and a comfy common room to relax in. Want to sit in the sun? Charles Morton Court has a peaceful garden space, perfect for when you need a break from studies. With on-site laundry, bicycle storage and high-speed broadband and WiFi included, you’re bound to feel right at home in no time.

The best part? Your accommodation is surrounded by delicious eateries, handy shops, and fabulous green spaces. So, whether it’s a quick meal and a cocktail after a long day, or a quiet walk through Newington Green – the choice is yours. Get ready to explore the big, bold city of London, all just a stone’s throw away from your luxury accommodation! Get this…our accommodation has contents insurance too, so sit back, relax, and chill out, because we’ve got you covered.

London Metropolitan University

London Metropolitan University is a modern institution focused on providing accessible and career-driven education. With a diverse range of programmes in fields such as business, health, and creative arts, it emphasises hands-on learning and professional development. Located across two campuses in London, the university offers a welcoming and inclusive environment for students from all backgrounds. Strong industry partnerships, work placements, and practical projects prepare graduates for the job market. London Met’s commitment to social mobility and student success makes it an ideal choice for those seeking an affordable and supportive university experience in one of the world’s most dynamic cities.

Central Saint Martins (UAL)

Central Saint Martins is a global leader in art and design education, celebrated for its cutting-edge innovation. As part of the University of the Arts London, it offers courses in fashion, fine art, and industrial design, encouraging students to push creative boundaries. Its King’s Cross campus provides state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant collaborative atmosphere. Known for producing pioneering graduates, Central Saint Martins integrates theory with practice, preparing students to challenge conventions and redefine creative industries. The school’s strong connections to London’s cultural scene and creative sectors position students at the forefront of artistic transformation.

City, University of London

City, University of London, is renowned for its professional focus and ties to business, law, and technology sectors. Located in the heart of London, it offers programmes designed to equip students with practical skills and a global perspective. The university is home to the prestigious Bayes Business School and has a strong reputation in journalism, engineering, and healthcare. With cutting-edge facilities and a focus on employability, City ensures graduates are industry-ready. Its central location provides unparalleled access to global organisations and a multicultural student experience, making it a prime choice for those seeking career-focused higher education.
